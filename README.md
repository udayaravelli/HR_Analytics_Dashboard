# HR_Analytics_Dashboard
HR Analytics Dashboard to uncover trends that can drive workforce retention and engagement strategies.

# 📊 Key Metrics Overview
- Total Employees:1,470 
- Attrition Rate: 16.09% 
- Average Age: 37 years 
- Average Salary: 6.5K 
- Average Tenure: 7 years 

# 💡 What the Data Reveals: 
1️⃣ Age & Attrition:
 - The 26-35 age group shows the highest attrition. 
 - Employees in their early career phases may need better growth opportunities and engagement. 

2️⃣ Salary Matters:
 - Attrition is most significant for employees earning <5K, with 163 employees leaving. 
 - Competitive compensation could help reduce turnover in this group. 

3️⃣ Early-Stage Attrition: 
 - Attrition spikes during the first 1-2 years of tenure. Strengthening onboarding and early-stage mentorship can make a difference. 

4️⃣ Job Satisfaction Impact: 
 - Employees with medium satisfaction account for the highest attrition. Focusing on initiatives that uplift satisfaction could reduce turnover. 

5️⃣ Role & Department Trends: 
 - Attrition is notable in Sales and R&D roles. Tailored support for these departments can help retain key talent. 

6️⃣ Diversity Focus:
 - Female employees in the 26-35 age group face significant attrition. Flexible policies and career support may address this.
